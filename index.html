About Me

Throughout my career at the intersection of business and technology, I've built my work around one core belief — the best technology solutions start with deeply understanding people — their workflows, their pain points, and the outcomes they're trying to achieve.

I work as a Product Owner and Senior Business Systems Analyst, specializing in Healthcare IT and Utility & Energy Efficiency — delivering solutions for some of the largest health insurers and energy efficiency programs in the country.
